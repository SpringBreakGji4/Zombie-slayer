2 X Rusty old Vehicles.

Hillbilly type vehicle has 141 polygons and 17 polygons per wheel.

Sedan type vehicle has 93 polygons and 16 polygons per wheel.

There is also a small shader script to show double sided normals.

Also included are 6 X original uncompressed textures in .TIF format.

Textures include Diffuse, Specular and Texture + Alpha for Specular in Unity.
